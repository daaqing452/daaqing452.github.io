About Me

Hello, I'm Yiqin Lu, a fourth-year Ph.D. student in Pervasive Human-Computer Interaction Group, Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Yuanchun Shi and Prof. Chun Yu. I received my Bachelor's degree at Department of Computer Science & Technology, Tsinghua University.

My research interests are input technologies on mobile devices like smartphones, smartwatches and head-mounted displays, in which I focus on the intention inference of users' low-effort inputs with multiple modalities like touch, gesture, gaze and so on.
